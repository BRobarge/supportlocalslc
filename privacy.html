Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SupportLocalSLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website, directories, dashboards, and related services (the "Services").

Information we collect

  • Account info: Email, password (hashed), and optional profile details you provide.
  • Business info: Content you submit for listings, offers, photos, and related metadata.
  • Usage data: Log data such as IP address, device/browser, pages viewed, and timestamps.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to keep you signed in and remember preferences.
  • OAuth data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic profile (name, email) from Google.

How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate users, secure accounts, and prevent abuse.
  • Display listings and offers you choose to publish.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, and service messages.
  • Analyze usage to improve performance and user experience.

Legal bases (where applicable)

Where required, we rely on legitimate interests to operate and improve the Services, contract performance to provide requested features, and consent (e.g., for certain cookies or email communications).

Sharing of information

  • Service providers: We use third parties to host, store, authenticate, and analyze (e.g., Supabase for authentication/database/storage; Google for OAuth). Providers may process data on our behalf under agreements that protect your information.
  • Business content: Information you publish on your listing or offers is visible to the public.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information to comply with law, enforce policies, or protect rights and safety.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use essential cookies for login/session management and may use analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you block essential cookies, some features may not work.

Data retention

We keep information while your account is active and for as long as needed to operate the Services, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account as described below.

Data security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • Access/Update: You can view and update profile data in your dashboard.
  • Delete: Request account deletion by contacting us. We may retain limited data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • Email preferences: You can opt out of non‑essential emails; you will still receive transactional/security emails.

Children’s privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13. If we learn a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it.

State privacy rights (U.S.)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the info below. We may need to verify your request.

International transfers

We are U.S.-based. If you access the Services from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. The date at the top shows the latest version. Material changes will be noted on this page.
