# SupportLocalSLC — Monetization Flywheel (Strategy Note)
*The business-model insight behind free vs. paid listings. This is a future SaaS pitch slide: "free listings monetize themselves by promoting paid members."*

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT
Free listings are not a cost — they are **advertising inventory** for paying members.

Every business page on the directory (including free/unclaimed ones created via the prospecting tool) is a page that:
1. Can rank in search (SEO surface area), and
2. Displays "Featured Nearby Offers" from PAYING businesses.

So the more businesses in the directory — paid OR free — the more surface area exists to promote the paying members. Free listings work FOR the paid members.

---

## THE TWO-SIDED INCENTIVE (the elegant part)
Upgrading from Free to Paid does TWO things at once:

**1. Removes ads from your OWN page.** Free pages show featured offers from other (paying) businesses. Paid pages are clean — just you. ("Stop subsidizing your competitors.")

**2. Puts YOU into the ad slots on everyone else's pages.** Paid businesses are the ones featured across the directory's free pages.

Single upsell, double benefit: *"Go Pro — no ads on your listing, plus your offers get featured across the directory."*

---

## THE STATUS ASYMMETRY (why people won't stay free)
- **Free** = you're listed, but you're carrying ads for paying members. You're working for them.
- **Paid** = ad-free, and others are now working for you.

This isn't just "unlock features." It's "stop working for your competitors and make them work for you." Much stronger psychological pull than a normal feature gate.

---

## THE RELEVANCE RULE (don't break consumer trust)
The featured-offers slots MUST be relevant + tasteful, or they poison the consumer habit that powers everything:
- Match category/sub-category and/or location (Italian restaurant page → other restaurant/Italian offers nearby)
- Rank paying businesses first among relevant matches
- 2-3 offers max, styled as a helpful "nearby deals" recommendation
- Never a banner farm

Relevant + limited + paid-prioritized = consumer gets value, paid member gets exposure, platform gets revenue. Everyone wins.

---

## WHY THIS COMPOUNDS
- Prospecting tool creates free listings at near-zero marginal cost → more inventory
- More inventory → more SEO pages + more ad surface for paid members
- More exposure for paid members → stronger reason to be paid → more upgrades
- More revenue → more outreach → more businesses → repeat

This is the standard platform flywheel (free users generate the surface that paid users pay to be promoted on), reverse-engineered for hyper-local directories.

---

## CONNECTION TO TIERS
- **Free Forever:** listed, claimable, can be shown ads, appears as inventory. Removes price objection, drives cold-start density.
- **Essentials ($49):** ad-free own page, can post offers, appears in some featured slots, follow system.
- **Pro ($99):** ad-free, max offers, top priority in featured slots across directory, featured/homepage placement, video, analytics.

The featured-offers placement is a concrete, ladder-able benefit that scales with tier.
