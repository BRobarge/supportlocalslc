Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 11, 2025

Overview

This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. We keep it simple and practical for a local directory.

What we collect

  • Account data: name, email, and auth data (via email/password or Google OAuth).
  • Business content: details you submit for your listing (texts, images, offers).
  • Usage data: basic logs and device info needed to run the site and keep it secure.
  • Pre-populated listings: we may seed listings from public sources and later verify with owners.

How we use data

  • Operate the site, show listings and offers, and enable claiming a business.
  • Communicate with you about your account or important changes.
  • Prevent abuse and keep the Service secure.

Service providers

We use trusted providers to run the Service:

  • Supabase (auth, database, storage, OAuth callback)
  • Vercel (site hosting and deployment)
  • Google (Google Sign-In, if enabled)

Cookies

We use essential cookies for login and basic site functionality. If we add analytics later, we will note it here and give you a clear opt-out.

Data sharing

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We share data with service providers only to operate the Service under our instructions.
  • We may disclose if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Retention

We keep account data while your account is active. We retain some records as needed for security, legal, or operational reasons.

Your choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account data by emailing us.
  • Unpublish or edit your business listing from your dashboard.
  • Request account deletion: email admin@robargemarketing.com with the subject “Delete my account.” We’ll verify and process it.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

State/region rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (for example, to know, access, correct, or delete data). We honor valid requests—email us and we’ll walk you through it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method is perfectly secure.

Changes

We may update this policy by posting a new “Last updated” date. If changes are significant, we’ll try to give you a heads up.

Contact

Questions? Email admin@robargemarketing.com.
